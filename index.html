About

Built in Rhode Island. Invested for the long term.

T&O Development Group is a Rhode Island–based real estate development and investment firm. We acquire, develop, and manage properties across the region, pairing institutional discipline with hands-on local execution.

Licensed and experienced, we invest alongside our partners and hold much of what we build. Our work is measured not by transactions, but by the lasting value we create for the communities and investors we serve.
